DDS Signal Generator

A signal generator for a home lab:

  • Frequency range: 20Hz - 30MHz
  • AD9951 DDS
  • 100MHz external crystal clock generator with 4x internal multiplication
  • PIC16F886 microcontroller
  • Rotary encoder
  • 16x2 LCD panel
  • Fs low-pass output filter

Note: all these projects are in progress, parts have been ordered, requirements can change.
