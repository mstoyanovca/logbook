DDS VFO

A DDS VFO for the 80m single band transceiver, based on the DDS signal generator. The differences are in the firmware and in the band-pass output filter. With the 14-bit DAC of the AD9951, the signal should be clean enough for a local oscillator, without the need of a multi-loop frequency synthesizer:

  • Frequency range: 5.000 - 5.500MHz
  • AD9951 DDS
  • PIC16F886 microcontroller
  • Optical rotary encoder
  • 16x2 LCD panel
  • Band-pass output filter
